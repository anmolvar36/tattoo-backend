# 01 - Business Requirements Document

## 1. Executive Summary
**Tattooplatz Zürich** is an innovative co-working tattoo studio based in Zurich, Switzerland. The platform serves as a B2B space-rental booking ecosystem allowing independent local and international guest tattoo artists to rent fully-equipped workstations on an hourly or session basis.

## 2. Core Business Objectives
- **Website Redesign:** Modernize the studio's digital web presence into a high-contrast, premium punk-chic web application.
- **Bespoke Booking Engine:** Replace the legacy SimplyBook system with a custom space-rental booking engine tailored specifically for workstation logistics.
- **0% Commission Policy:** The studio charges zero commission on artist earnings from their clients—artists only pay for station rental time.
- **E-Commerce Store:** Rebuild and modernize the studio merchandise storefront (Vulva Edition, Minimal Edition apparel & accessories).
- **Smooth Data Migration:** Preserve historical records and scheduled appointments during the transition from SimplyBook.

## 3. Station Rental & Operating Specifications
- **Station Count:** Currently 4 bookable workstations (expandable to 6+ in settings).
- **Operating Days & Hours:** Wednesday to Sunday, 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM (11:00 to 18:00). Monday and Tuesday are closed.
- **Minimum Booking Duration:** 2 hours.
- **Standard Pricing Packages:**
  - 1 Hour Session: 33.00 CHF
  - 3 Hour Session: 90.00 CHF
  - 4 Hour Session: 120.00 CHF
  - 6 Hour Session: 170.00 CHF / 180.00 CHF
  - 8 Hour Session: 220.00 CHF
- **Included Equipment:** Hydraulic chairs, rolling trays, LED ring lights, surface disinfectants, paper towels, stencil printers, Wi-Fi, coffee machine.
- **Artist Responsibilities:** Artists must bring their own tattoo machine, needles, inks, cartridges, and personal supplies.

## 4. Legal & Compliance Policy
- **EU/EFTA Citizens:** Can work up to 90 days without formal work visa but must register via EasyGov Swiss portal at least 8 days prior to working.
- **Non-EU Nationals:** Direct contact with legal team required via Instagram or Email.
- **Compliance Terms (No Customer Uploads):** Customers/artists do NOT upload compliance documents during registration or booking. Instead, they acknowledge and accept compliance terms via a mandatory checkbox during checkout. Official hygiene declarations and compliance records are uploaded and managed exclusively by studio administration in the Admin Dashboard.
